# Electrical Safety & Fire Safety (Diploma Level)

## Purpose
This repository documents my learning and practical understanding of
electrical safety and electrical fire safety as a Diploma Electrical Engineer.
This is the foundation for factory work, ABC Electrician License, and compliance.

## What I Am Learning

### 1. Electrical Safety
- PPE (helmet, gloves, shoes, arc protection)
- Electric shock hazards
- Lockout Tagout (LOTO) – basic procedure
- Safe isolation practice

### 2. Electrical Fire Safety
- Short circuit fire
- Overload fire
- Loose connection fire
- CO₂ and DCP fire extinguisher usage
- Electrical fire prevention methods

### 3. Basic Wiring & Earthing
- House wiring safety rules
- Plate earthing
- Pipe earthing
- Basic earthing resistance concept

### 4. ABC Electrician License Preparation
- Electrical symbols
- Safety rules
- Basic wiring questions
- Simple motor and earthing questions
